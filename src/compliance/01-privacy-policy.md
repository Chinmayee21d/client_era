# Client Era Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 2026**

Client Era ("we," "us," or "our") operates the clientera.com B2B operations and distributor management platform. This Privacy Policy details how we handle the personal and corporate data we collect when you use our platform.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Operational Data
Because we are a B2B platform, the primary data we collect is related to supply chain and operational efficiency:
- **Corporate Contact Info:** Names, email addresses, and phone numbers of authorized users (distributors, sales managers, dispatchers).
- **Transactional Records:** Sales orders (SORs), proforma invoices, pricing agreements, and fulfillment tracking logs.

### B. Technical Data
- IP addresses, browser types, and device identifiers required to secure the platform.
- System activity logs, session durations, and interaction metrics used to optimize platform functionality.

## 2. How We Use Information

Client Era processes data to:
- Authenticate authorized users and enforce role-based access controls across our modules.
- Facilitate B2B commerce (e.g., executing SORs, notifying distributors of ETA updates).
- Maintain service security, prevent fraud, and maintain comprehensive audit trails.
- Analyze operational bottlenecks to improve our software routing and algorithms.

## 3. Data Sharing and Sub-processors

We do not sell operational or personal data to third parties. We only share information with:
- **Sub-processors:** Essential infrastructure providers (e.g., AWS, Vercel) bound by strict data processing agreements.
- **Your Organization:** Actions performed by users (e.g., a Sales Manager) are visible to authorized personnel within the same organization based on defined permissions.
- **Legal Compliance:** Law enforcement or regulatory bodies, only when legally required.

## 4. Data Security

Client Era employs enterprise-grade security, including:
- Encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
- Strict infrastructure access controls and role-based operational permissions.
- Regular vulnerability scanning and independent security audits.

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you or your organization may have the right to:
- Access, rectify, or erase your personal operational data.
- Withdraw consent for non-essential data processing.
- Request an export of your transaction history.

To exercise these rights, please contact your organization's Platform Administrator or email us at **privacy@clientera.com**.
