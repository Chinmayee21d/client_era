# Client Era Cookie Policy

**Last Updated: March 2026**

Client Era uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to ensure our distributor platform functions securely and efficiently. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the platform to remember your actions, session states, and preferences over time, so you don't have to keep re-entering them.

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### A. Strictly Necessary Cookies (Always Active)
These cookies are essential for the core operation of the Client Era platform.
- **Purpose:** User authentication, security tokens, load balancing, and maintaining session state during enterprise workflows.
- **Control:** You cannot disable these cookies within our systems, as the platform will not function without them. You can configure your browser to block them, but the platform will break.

### B. Analytical/Performance Cookies
These cookies help us understand how internal and external users interact with the platform.
- **Purpose:** Analyzing page load times, tracking the usage of the Dispatch terminal, and optimizing overall software performance.
- **Tooling:** We may use customized analytics tools that only process aggregated, anonymized data.

### C. Functional Cookies
These cookies allow the platform to provide enhanced, personalized functionality.
- **Purpose:** Remembering your dashboard layout preferences, dark/light mode toggles, and localized timezone settings.

## 3. Do Not Track (DNT)

If your browser sends a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal, Client Era respects it by automatically disabling non-essential Analytical and Functional cookies. 

## 4. How to Manage Your Cookies

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Preferences" link located in the footer of our website. This will re-open the consent banner, allowing you to accept or reject non-essential cookies.

For questions about this policy, contact us at **privacy@clientera.com**.
