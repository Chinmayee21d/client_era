## My Reading of the Page's Job

This is **not** a generic SaaS marketing page. The audience is a **Sales Manager, Regional Director, or Business Owner** at an Indian B2B company — someone currently running their team on WhatsApp groups, Excel sheets, and end-of-day verbal updates. They feel the pain daily. The page needs to speak directly to that pain and show them a better world.

The pitch deck's core line says it best: *"Old systems record what happened. ClientEra decides what should happen next — and executes it."*

The Sales Management page specifically needs to answer **3 questions** in the visitor's head:
1. **"Do you understand my problem?"** — The visibility gap. Managing blind.
2. **"What exactly do you give me?"** — The specific features per management level.
3. **"Will this actually work for my team?"** — Proof through specificity, real scenarios, real numbers.
